My name is Reihane Boghrati. I'm currently a post-doctoral researcher in marketing department of Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania. I've received my Ph.D in Computer Science from University of Southern California in 2018. I enjoy designing and applying machine learning and natural language processing methods to investigate psychological phenomena expressed in written and spoken language.
